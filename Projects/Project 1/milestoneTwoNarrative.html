Reflect on the process of enhancing and modifying the artifact. What did you learn as you were creating it and improving it? What challenges did you face?

While enhancing and modifying the artifact I had to determine exactly how I wanted the different screens to be designed. For the login screen, I had to decide if I wanted the login and account creation processes to be together on one screen or separately on two different screens. I decided to make them separate because I felt the application flows better this way. Additionally, the most challenging portion of enhancing and modifying the original artifact was designing the gameplay screen. I had the idea to make this a retro-style game since it is text-based, so I went with a retro-style look. When creating this look, determining the size and placement for each component took a good amount of thought. However, this critical thinking allowed me to learn the comfortable placement of screen components for a mobile application and will certainly benefit me in the future.
